# Image Specifications — Jackson Group Website

The team asked for the specs of every image slot so photography can be sized
correctly. All images should be **JPG (photos) or transparent PNG (logos)**,
sRGB, and under **500 KB** after compression where possible (use
[squoosh.app](https://squoosh.app) or similar). Supply at the sizes below —
larger is fine (we scale down, never up).

## Logos

| Slot | Size (px) | Format | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Jackson Group logo | SVG preferred, else PNG ≥ 600×160 | SVG / PNG transparent | Used in header & footer on dark background |
| Client logos | 1080×1080 | PNG transparent | Already supplied — this spec is for any additions |

## Case study / work imagery

| Slot | Size (px) | Ratio | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Work tile (grid) | 1200×900 | 4:3 | Shown darkened behind the client name; avoid text in the image |
| Case study page hero/inline | 1600×1200 (landscape) or 1200×1500 (portrait) | 4:3 / 4:5 | Campaign creative, event photos — portrait posters like the PigaBet artwork work well |

## About / general photography

| Slot | Size (px) | Ratio | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Full-width section background | 2400×1350 | 16:9 | Keep the key subject centre-right; a dark gradient is overlaid on the left |
| Team / office photos | 1200×1500 | 4:5 | Consistent lighting across the set reads best |

## Video

| Slot | Spec | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Campaign clips | MP4 (H.264), 1080p, ≤ 15 MB, ≤ 60 s | Portrait reels are fine for work pages; landscape for full-width sections |

## General guidance

- Real campaign/event photography over stock — the feedback on the draft site
  made clear that generic stock imagery should go.
- Photos will often sit under a dark overlay with white text on top, so
  mid-tone images with clear subjects work better than very busy or very
  bright ones.
